ABOUT US

The villa is located in the central part of the island of Ammoulian, about 200m from the sea and unorganized beach at the tavern "Janis". The most famous beach on the island of Alikes can be reached in less than 15 minutes by easy walking. About 70m from the villa is the most basic market on the island of Panagora and the bakery "Rodolfos". The very appearance of villa Filiko I and the ambience surrounding the villa leaves the impression of traditional Greek villas. Facade decorated with stone, garden with greenery, sitting set, summer house and barbecue, complement the content of the villa. The villa contains spacious studios, comfortable apartments and duplexes, very carefully decorated. Due to the varied selection of accommodation units, Villa is ideal for families with children and for larger groups. The villa has a reception desk, which is also a shared luggage storage room on the day of the shift. The villa is located in a quiet part of the island in a non-residential street, so it is possible to park a car on the street in front of the villa or near it.
